# Developer Persona

The developer persona is the original BrainTemplate experience. Skills and templates here are unchanged from pre-persona BrainTemplate (Plan 3 will repackage with persona-specific vocabulary, profile-skeleton, etc.).

If you picked this persona during setup, your vault is configured for code projects with `~/Development/<slug>/` external folders, `project_files/brain/` symlinks into Brain, and the full developer skill bundle.

See `_HowThisWorks.md` at the vault root for architecture details.
